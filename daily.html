Hidden Gem + Verse of the Day—then a practical step to live it out.

Hidden Gem

First Things First

When you reorder your loves, your life follows. Give God the first word and the last word today.

Matthew 6:33
“But seek first the kingdom of God and His righteousness, and all these things will be added to you.”

Reflection

Put God first in attention, time, and trust. Start and end with Him today—then let everything else find its place.

One Step

Begin and end your day by praying this: “Lord, reorder my loves. Help me seek You first.”
