30-Day “Seek First” Plan

A daily journey to put the Kingdom first (Matthew 6:33). Read, reflect, respond — one small step each day.

Read: Daily scripture
Focus: Theme for the day
Prompt: One question
Action: A small, concrete step
How to Use

Each day: Read the passage, Focus your heart, answer the Prompt, take the Action. Check “Done” to save progress locally.

Weekly Rhythm

End of each week, share one testimony and one challenge with a friend or group. Pray short, specific prayers.

Prayer to Begin

“Father, today I seek Your Kingdom first. Align my heart, reorder my desires, and lead my steps. Amen.”

Anchor Verse

“But seek first the kingdom of God and his righteousness, and all these things will be added to you.” — Matthew 6:33
