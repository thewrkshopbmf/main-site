✅ Habit Checklist

A simple weekly rhythm — Faith, Family, Work.

Faith

Open the Word daily; note one truth to carry.
Name thanks, needs, and next step in obedience.
Send one word of Scripture-anchored encouragement.

Family

Phone down; be fully present with loved ones.
Affirm identity, effort, and growth you see.
A simple act that lifts someone’s load.

Work

Define the top three outcomes for the week.
Choose honesty, diligence, and stewardship.
Review, capture next steps, close loops.

Tip: progress is saved in your browser so you can return to it during the week.
